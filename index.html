A CHRISTMAS
FAIRYLAND AT
THE CASTLE OF
GRAND-BIGARD

The award-winning Lightopia comes to Belgium this
Winter, to the historic park of the Castle of Grand-Bigard.
Promising to be Belgium's finest festival of lights and
lanterns this Christmas. See the castle reborn in light,
alongside incredible installations, immersive light routes
and a stunning water show.

A FAIRY CHRISTMAS
CELEBRATION

The Christmas Fairyland trail will take its audience on a
magical 2 km journey, with 100 different groups of
lantern and light installations of LED, offering the
ultimate multisensory experience. This winter Lightopia
will bring never-before- seen bespoke light installations
to Belgium, and for the first time ever, will showcase an
interactive Tree of Life. Protected by the Manneken Pis
Fairy, the Tree of Life will dazzle guests with colourful
illuminations, specifically designed for Belgium.

Come to see Europe's largest Christmas light display,
with immersive installations designed to delight families,
couples and friends alike this Christmas.
